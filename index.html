Pre-Construction

& Contracting

Bid it, contract it, sign it — in one unbroken flow.

This is where most real estate projects fall apart — vague scopes, unverified contractors, bids that can't be compared, and contracts that leave the investor exposed. Project Prep Pro closes every gap in a single connected flow. Send a fully detailed Scope of Work to any subcontractor's phone or email via secure magic link — no account required on their end. Choose from five tiers of due diligence scaled to the size of the contract: a basic ID check for a small job, a full license, insurance, and financial background check before you hand over a six-figure commitment. Compare every bid apples-to-apples, award the winner, and the platform auto-generates the contract pre-filled with all variables and ready for e-signature. Lender SOW, project schedule, and compliance tracking are all built into the same flow — nothing lives in a separate tool.

  • Magic link bidding — subs submit without creating an account
  • 5-tier due diligence: basic ID verification to full financial background check
  • Automated contract generation + e-signature, pre-filled from the winning bid
  • Lender SOW with draw schedule detail + Gantt schedule builder
  • Compliance tracker — contract status, document receipt, custom checkboxes
05

Construction

& Execution

Real-time visibility into budget, progress, and every dollar spent.

Most projects go over budget not because of bad planning — but because there is no system catching problems in real time. By the time an investor finds out a trade is 20% over, the money is already spent. Project Prep Pro puts you inside the project without being on site. A live dashboard compares budgeted costs to actuals and tracks timeline plan against real progress, updated continuously. Subcontractors must submit change orders with required photos and a written description before the request reaches your budget — no more surprise invoices after the fact. Daily site logs and field execution tracking keep every party accountable. Live camera integration is available for remote monitoring. Integrates with QuickBooks so your books stay current without a second entry.

  • Real-time budget vs. actuals with custom dashboard builder
  • Change order approval — photo and written description required before any budget impact
  • Daily site logs and upcoming work scheduling
  • QuickBooks integration + live on-site camera option
  • Full accounting and job-costing ledger built in

Post-Sale

& Archive

Close the deal. Make the next one more profitable.

Most investors close a deal and move on — leaving the data, the cost variances, and the hard-won lessons scattered across emails, memory, and a spreadsheet nobody updates. Every deal you close without capturing what happened is a deal that doesn't make the next one better. Project Prep Pro closes that loop. A financial post-mortem compares every projected number against every actual — margin, timeline, cost by trade and line item — and formats the results for investors, partners, or internal review. Vendor closeout collects lien waivers and contractor paperwork in one structured flow. The friction log and pricing adjustment engine capture where estimates drifted and why, then feed those corrections directly back into your underwriting model. Over time, your projections get more accurate with every deal you close — because the platform learns from all of them.

  • Financial post-mortem — projected vs. actual by stage, trade, and line item

  • Friction log — structured retrospective organized by project phase
