Know If the Deal Works Before You Make the Offer

AI underwriting pulls live public data and calculates your real numbers — ARV, renovation cost, holding cost, projected margin — in minutes. Stop making offers on gut instinct. Start making offers backed by a system that has seen every variable.

Field Data → SOW → Bid → Signed Contract. Zero Re-Entry.

Field intelligence from the property populates your Scope of Work automatically. The SOW feeds the bid package. The winning bid generates the contract — pre-filled, ready for e-signature. No spreadsheet handoffs. No re-entering the same data four times. The chain runs end to end without you touching it twice.

Bids Back in 24 Hours — No Sub Account Required

Send a fully detailed Scope of Work to any subcontractor's phone or email via secure link in under 60 seconds. They review and bid online without creating an account. Every bid comes back structured and comparable. The days of chasing subs for a number are over.

Construction Visibility Without Being on Site

A live dashboard compares budget to actuals continuously. Change orders require photos and a written description before they touch your budget — no more surprise invoices after the fact. Integrates with QuickBooks. Live camera option available. You know what is happening on every site without a single phone call.
